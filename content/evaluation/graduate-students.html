--- title: "Graduate Students" date: "Updated 2021-03-05" ---

Graduate students have several additional requirements for the course.

Final Report

For the final report, graduate students must prepare a report that is related to their thesis or dissertation research.

Additional Project

Graduate students must additionally complete one of the following additional projects. Undergraduate students may complete these requirements for additional credit.

Professional Website

Students must create and deploy a website highlighting their skills, experience, and professional portfolio, as appropriate to their professional goals.

R Package, Shiny App, Web Tutorial, or Other Resource

Students must create and deploy an R package or web-based resource for a topic of interest. This may include a blog post analyzing a dataset and presenting the results; a web tutorial on an analytic, programming, visualization, experimental, or workflow method; a Shiny app implementing a method of interest; or another topic.

Schedule

The due dates for these additional projects will be announced.
